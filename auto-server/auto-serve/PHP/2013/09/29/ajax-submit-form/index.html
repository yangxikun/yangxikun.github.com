Ajax提交表单

29 September 2013

主要使用了jQuery ajax - serialize() 方法.

The .serialize() method creates a text string in standard URL-encoded notation.

It can act on a jQuery object that has selected individual form controls, such as <input>, <textarea>, and <select>: $( "input, textarea, select" ).serialize();

html代码:

 1 <html>
 2     <head>
 3         <script src="http://code.jquery.com/jquery-1.9.1.js"></script>
 4         <script>
 5             $(function(){
 6                 $('#myForm').on('submit',function(e){
 7                     $.ajax({
 8                         url:'./ajaxSubmit.php',
 9                         data:$(this).serialize(),
10                         type:'POST',
11                         success:function(result){
12                             $('#var_dump_POST').append(result);
13                         }
14                     });
15                 return false;
16             });
17             });
18         </script>
19     </head>
20     <body>
21         <form action="" method="post" id="myForm">
22             <label for="username">username:</label>
23             <input type="text" name="username">
24             <input type="submit">
25         </form>
26         <div id="var_dump_POST"></div>
27     </body>
28 </html>

2     var_dump($_POST);
3 ?>
